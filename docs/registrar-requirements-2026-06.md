## 1. Form 137 vs SF 10 — which form, which years

Both are the **permanent record**. "Form 137" is the OLD name; "SF 10" is the new name.
SF 10 replaced Form 137 on a **staggered, per-grade-level basis** per **DepEd Order
No. 58, s. 2017** (SF-10 Effectivity Chart). Earliest grades adopted SF-10 first.

Effectivity chart (E = Elementary F137-E, A = F137-A for HS; otherwise SF-10):

| Grade | 17-18 | 18-19 | 19-20 | 20-21 | 21-22 | 22-23 |
|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| 1  | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 |
| 2  | F137-E| SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 |
| 3  | F137-E| F137-E| SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 |
| 4  | F137-E| F137-E| F137-E| SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 |
| 5  | F137-E| F137-E| F137-E| F137-E| SF-10 | SF-10 |
| 6  | F137-E| F137-E| F137-E| F137-E| F137-E| SF-10 |
| 7  | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 |
| 8  | F137-A| SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 |
| 9  | F137-A| F137-A| SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 |
| 10 | F137-A| F137-A| F137-A| SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 |
| 11 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 |
| 12 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 | SF-10 |

Implications:
- ALL students used **4 quarters** up to and including **SY 2025-26**.
- Starting **SY 2026-27**: **3 terms** + SF-10 only.
- A single learner's record can mix F137 (old grade-years) and SF-10 (new grade-years).
- Form 137 is **still issued on request** when the requester asks for "Form 137"
  (for old records). SF-10 is issued for SF-10 requests. The two outputs differ in
  some entries (see #2).
- System should pick the correct form template per **grade-level + school-year** using
  the chart above.

## 2. SF 10 has fields Form 137 didn't
- SF 10 includes **District** and **Region**.
- SF 10 has a **teacher signature**.
  - For **NPS teachers**: affix the signature.
  - For SF 10 **received from another school**: leave signature blank, just **type the
    teacher's name**.

## 3. Encoding SF 10 received FROM other schools
- No screen yet to enter/update entries from an **externally received SF 10**.
- Need a page to record transferred-in records (their old subjects/grades/sections,
  per-year, keeping the origin school's subject names) — name typed, signature blank.

## 4. Credentials Submitted checklist — changes
Current list: BC, BP, HC, Pix, RF, F137 (transferees only), Certification/Report Card (RC), GMC.
Requested changes:
- **Split** "Certification / Report Card" into two separate items:
  - **Report Card** (its own checkbox)
  - **Certification** → rename to **Certification of Eligibility**
- **Add** these (can live under an **"Others"** grouping):
  - ESC Certificate / Voucher
  - Diploma
  - Affidavit of Undertaking
  - Confirmation Certificate
- Add a free-text **"Others"** field at the end — some learners have extra
  requirements not in the fixed list.

## 5. Previous School Attended — for ALL transferees, every level
- Today there is only "**Elementary graduated from…**" (fine for Grade 7+).
- Need **Previous School Attended** for **all transferees**, including:
  - Elementary transferees (currently no field for them).
  - Grade 11 transferees need where they **finished Grade 10**.
- Don't restrict the "previous school" concept to elementary completion only.

## 6. School of origin / NPS-origin flag
- Issue: a learner who has been at NPS since e.g. Grade 6 (Abad, Bien) shows
  "first yr in NPS" but should clearly read **NPS**.
- Want it **automatic**: if the learner originates from NPS (parochial graduate),
  show "NPS" and **exclude them from the transferees list**.
- Only true transferees (from other schools) should appear as transferees / require
  "previous school attended".

## 7. Birthday list per month (for SCO)
- New report: list of students with **birthdays per month**.
- Requested by SCO for their use.

## 8. Listings grouped by sex
- Class/learner listings (e.g. **Form 1 / SF 1** and similar) must always be
  **grouped Male first, then Female** (DepEd convention).

## 9. Alumni list = previous years' graduates
- Current "Alumni" only shows **this year's graduating** learners.
- Need the real **alumni list = graduates of previous school years**.

## 10. Loyalty Awardees (Grade 12) — Gold AND Silver
- Grade 12 loyalty awards have **Gold** and **Silver** tiers.
- Current system lists **Gold** only (continuous since enrollment).
- **Silver** = started Grade 10 through Grade 12 — **not in system yet**. Add it.

## 11. "Saan magdadag ng subject?" — Add Subject screen
- Registrar needs a clear place to **add/manage subjects** (per grade level / SY).
